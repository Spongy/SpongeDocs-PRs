=================
Developing Sponge
=================

In this guide are pages discussing development on portions of Sponge, the API, coremod and implementation. Please read the guidelines first before contributing, otherwise your contribution may be denied, if not delayed.

.. note::

    This guide shows topics targetted specifically at Sponge contributors.
    
    If you are a server owner, see the :doc:`../../users/index` for information on how to use Sponge.
    
    If you are a plugin developer, see :doc:`../../plugins/index` for information about writing Sponge plugins.
